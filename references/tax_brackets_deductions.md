## Capital Gains Tax Rates (<!-- YEAR -->2025<!-- /YEAR -->)

### Long-Term Capital Gains (held > 1 year)

<!-- LTCG_TABLE -->
| Filing Status | 0% Rate | 15% Rate | 20% Rate |
|---------------|---------|----------|----------|
| Single | Up to $48,350 | $48,351 - $533,400 | Over $533,400 |

| Married Filing Separately | Up to $48,350 | $48,351 - $300,000 | Over $300,000 |
| Head of Household | Up to $64,750 | $64,751 - $566,700 | Over $566,700 |
<!-- /LTCG_TABLE -->

**Short-term capital gains**: Taxed as ordinary income

**Net Investment Income Tax (NIIT)**: 3.8% additional tax on investment income when MAGI exceeds:
<!-- NIIT_THRESHOLDS -->
- Single: $200,000
- Married Filing Jointly: $250,000
- Married Filing Separately: $125,000
<!-- /NIIT_THRESHOLDS -->

## Self-Employment Tax (<!-- YEAR -->2025<!-- /YEAR -->)

<!-- SE_TAX -->
### Social Security
- Rate: 12.4% (employer + employee portions)
- Wage base: $176,100
- Maximum tax: $21,838

### Medicare
- Rate: 2.9% (employer + employee portions)
- No wage limit
- Additional 0.9% on earnings over $200,000 (single) / $250,000 (MFJ)
<!-- /SE_TAX -->
